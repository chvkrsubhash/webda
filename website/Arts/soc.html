Sociology

Sociology is the scientific study of society, human social behavior, patterns of social relationships, social interaction, and aspects of culture associated with everyday life. It uses various methods of empirical investigation and critical analysis to develop a body of knowledge about social order and social change.

Sociology is a broad discipline that encompasses a wide range of topics, including:

  • Social stratification and inequality
  • Social institutions, such as the family, education, and religion
  • Social movements and collective behavior
  • Deviance and crime
  • Gender and sexuality
  • Race and ethnicity
  • Urbanization and globalization
  • Social change
